About Me

I consider myself to be a very creative person and have always been extremely passionate about music, technology, art, and design. For many years now I've worked my passion for media creation into several aspects of my career path.

Starting over 20 years ago with my introduction to print design, which lead to a Graphic Artist position in TV News Production for 10 years. Currently I have many roles at an independent record label, where I've spent the last 10 years as a Studio Engineer and Label Manager.
